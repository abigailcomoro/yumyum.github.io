  • Chicken Adobo

Yum Yum Kitchen Fresh food done right!

Ingredients

for 4 servings

Chicken and Marinade

  • 750g / 1.5 lb chicken thigh fillets
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • ⅓ cup soy sauce (all purpose or light, not dark soy sauce)
  • ⅓ cup white vinegar
  • 4 bay leaves

For Cooking

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small brown onion, diced
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 tbsp whole black pepper

Garnishing

  • 2 green onions, sliced

Abodo Cooking Instructions

  1. Combine chicken and marinade in a bowl to marinate at least 20 minutes or overnight.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in skillet over high heat. Remove chicken when ready (reserve marinade) and place in the pan. Sear both sides until brown on each side. Do not cook chicken all the way.
  3. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside
  4. Heat remaining oil in skillet and add garlic and onion.
  5. Add reserved marinade, water, sugar, and black pepper and bring to simmer then turn down heat to a medium-high.
  6. Add chicken and simmer uncovered for about 20 minutes, turning the chicken at around 15 minutesuntil sauce reduces down to a thicker consistency.
  7. Serve over rice and enjoy!
