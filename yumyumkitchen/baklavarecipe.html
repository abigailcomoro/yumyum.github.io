Ingredients

makes 24 pieces

Phyllo Pastry

  • Phyllo dough (found in the freezer section)

Nut Mixture

  • 6 oz shelled pistachios, coarsely chopped
  • 6 oz walnuts, coarsely chopped
  • 6 oz hazelnuts, coarsely chopped
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 to 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • large pinch of ground cloves
  • 1 ½ to 2 sticks of unsalted butter, melted

Honey Syrup

  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 tsp orange extract (optional)
  • 5 whole cloves
  • 1 lemon, juiced

Baklava Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven for 350 ° F (175 ° C)
  2. Make the Honey Syrup: Place the sugar and water in saucepan and heat stove-top, stirring occasionally until sugar dissolves. Add honey, orange extract (if using), and whole cloves; stir to mix. Bring to boil, then lower heat and let simmer for about 25 minutes. Remove syrup from heat and let cool til lukewarm. Add lemon juice and remove the whole cloves.
  3. Make the Nut Mixture: In the bowl of a food processor, add the pistachios, walnut, and hazelnuts. Pulse a few times to chop. Transfer to a large mixing bowl and add sugar, cinnamon, and ground gloves. Mix well to combine.
  4. Prepare Phyllo Pastry: Unroll the thawed phyllo pastry and place the sheets in between two clean kitchen towels. this will help the phyllo from breaking while you work.
  5. Assemble the Baklava: Prepare a 9"x13"x2" baking pan. Brush the interior of the baking pan with some of the melted butter.
  6. Take one sheet phyllo sheet and brush it with melted butter. Repeat the process until stacked about ⅓ of the pan.
  7. Distribute about ½ of the nut mixture over the layer and repeat between the phyllo sheet and nut mixture. Add remaining phyllo sheet is left and brush it with butter
  8. Using a shape knife, cut pastry into 24 diamond shaped (or squared) pieces.
  9. Place baking dish in middle rack and bake anywhere from 35 to 45 minutes or until the top of the baklava turns golden.
  10. When finished, still hot, pour honey syrup over baklava
  11. Let cool and enjoy!
